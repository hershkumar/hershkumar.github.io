Hello! My name is Hersh Kumar and I am an undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, studying Physics and Computer Science.

My interests mainly lie in quantum computing, and I am currently working on generating circuits for specific Hamiltonian models, for use in simulation of lattice gauge theories.

For personal projects, I have written [csolver](https://github.com/hershkumar/csolver), a small order arbitrary controlled NOT gate circuit finder, and am currently working on [tlon](https://github.com/hershkumar/tlon), a terminal based quantum circuit simulator. If you have any questions or find one of my (many) typos, feel free to reach out to me at `hershkumar13@gmail.com`.
